Smart Hostel Management

A complete hostel management platform built with PHP & MySQL. Role-based access for admins and students — rooms, fees, complaints, and more.

Everything You Need

Powerful features for both administrators and students.

🏠

Room Management

Add, edit, and delete rooms with gender-specific allocation. Track capacity, available beds, and room assignments in real time.

💰

Fee Payments

Configurable fee breakdown — room rent, mess fee, and maintenance. Detailed receipts with automatic expiry extension.

📋

Complaint Tracking

Students submit complaints with categories. Admins review, accept, or reject — with full status tracking and dashboard stats.

👨‍💼

Admin Dashboard

Complete control panel — manage students, rooms, allocations, notices, complaints, and room requests from one place.

🎓

Student Portal

Personal dashboard with room details, fee payment, receipt viewing, complaint submission, and notice board.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Separate login flows and dashboards for admins and students. Secure session-based authentication throughout.
